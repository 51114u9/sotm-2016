About the event

State of the Map Latam is the annual conference for all mappers and OpenStreetMap users in Latin America.

Schedule

The event will last three consecutive days in November 2016:

  • 25th and 26th - at Mário de Andrade Library: a public facility in downtown São Paulo, located in a place with good accommodation options and easy access to public transportation. All the talks will be held at the main conference room and there will be also a adjoint space for "Birds of a Feathers" sessions and workshops for aspiring mappers.
  • 27th - at Mobilab: On the last day there will be a mapathon at Mobilab, a urban mobility laboratory maintained by the Prefecture of São Paulo, also in downtown.
